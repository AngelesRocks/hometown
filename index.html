Visit Manta

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district near the most beautiful beaches in the south of Latin America.

Top three activities to do at Manta

Go swimming

Manta's beaches are the hottest for swimming with surfers and getting a beautiful tan.

Visit Latin America's largest port

Manta has the largest and most relevant seaport in Latin America, it is ideal for meeting foreigners from all over the world.

Go for a volleyball lesson

Manta stands out among the countries with the best beach sports, without a doubt the girls volleyball team stands out among them all!
